How to make small talk!

Ever feel like you just can't do small talk? I know I have. After years of hard work, I have figured out how to make small talk. And good news, I will share what I have learned with you!

Step 1: Establish Interest

Pick one of the pillars of polite topics you feel comfortable talking about. Discover your conversation partner's feelings on the topic. Pick an opposing side to the topic. See resource "Punnett Square Example" for more information.
     

Step 2: Dig Trenches

No matter what, never waiver on your stance in the conversation.
     

Step 3: End on a Hight Note

To end the conversation say, "yeah ok", roll your eyes, and walk away.
